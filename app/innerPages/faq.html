Getting Started


What do I need to use motiv8?

Here’s what you need to get started:

  • A computer or laptop running the desktop version of Chrome, Firefox or Opera
  • MetaMask, a digital wallet used specifically with web apps
  • Ether, a form of digital payment that powers motiv8

Installing MetaMask, your digital wallet

To use motib8, you will need to install MetaMask, a digital wallet. You will need to put money in it to make your first purchase. Note: A digital wallet like MetaMask acts like a bank account—treat it with respect and make sure you don’t forget your password or the seed words.

Why is MetaMask locked?

Occasionally motiv8 page displays a lock screen. This happens because MetaMask locks your account after a certain period of time automatically. To unlock simply click on the MetaMask extension and type in your password.
